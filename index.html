What are Fighter Jets?

Fighter jets are high-speed, agile, and heavily armed military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat and air-to-ground attacks. They are crucial for air superiority and national defense.

Types of Fighter Jets

F-22 Raptor

The F-22 Raptor is a fifth-generation fighter aircraft designed for air superiority and stealth. It is capable of high-speed maneuvers and stealth technology.

SU-57 Felon

The SU-57 Felon is a Russian stealth multirole fighter designed for air-to-air and air-to-ground combat. It features cutting-edge avionics and speed.

Dassault Rafale

The Dassault Rafale is a French fighter jet designed for versatility and multi-role capabilities, excelling in both air superiority and ground attack missions.
